Buyer psychology: the coalition you need to close

Enterprise AI purchases are rarely won by one stakeholder. They are won when the economic buyer, technical owner, and risk gatekeeper can each see their concerns answered in the delivery model.

Economic buyer

Wants one workflow, one KPI, one proof path, and a realistic ROI hypothesis grounded in current operations.

Technical buyer

Wants integration clarity, release discipline, operating ownership, and a clear explanation of where the agent can and cannot act.

Risk gatekeeper

Wants approval design, audit evidence, fallback paths, and confidence that the workflow stays inside policy boundaries.

What this means for site content

  • Service pages should frame a narrow operational outcome and delivery sequence
  • Guide pages should explain governance, evaluation, and launch criteria in concrete terms
  • Insight pages should help buyers recognize failure modes and implementation tradeoffs before the first call
