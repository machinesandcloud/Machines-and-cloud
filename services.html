Pricing

Predictable fees, transparent consumption

Our pricing reflects two realities: buyers want predictable fees when outcomes are uncertain, and underlying providers price on consumption (tokens/credits).

Fixed-Fee Packages

Sprint, Readiness Kit, and PoV builds are fixed-fee. This reduces sales friction and forces scope discipline for both sides.

Milestone-Based Launch

Production Launch is milestone-based with payments tied to acceptance gates. You pay for results, not hours.

Transparent Pass-Through

Model and provider consumption is passed through with pre-agreed caps and alerts. No hidden markups on API costs.
