Regional Bank Cuts KYC Case Research Time by 67%

Agentic automation for compliance investigation workflows with full audit trails

The Challenge

A regional bank with $12B in assets faced mounting pressure on their compliance team. KYC case research was taking an average of 45 minutes per case, with analysts spending most of their time gathering data from multiple systems before making decisions.

The compliance team needed to process 200+ cases daily, but manual research was creating backlogs and risking regulatory deadlines. Previous automation attempts using RPA had failed due to the unstructured nature of the research process.

The Solution

We deployed an agentic workflow that automates the research phase of KYC investigations. The agent connects to core banking, external databases, and document repositories to gather relevant information and prepare case summaries for analyst review.

  • Integration with 5 internal systems via secure APIs
  • External data enrichment from approved vendor sources
  • Structured case summary generation with citations
  • Human approval gates before any case escalation
  • Complete audit trail for regulatory examination

Results

67% Reduction in research time
15 min Average case completion (from 45 min)
0 Compliance findings in first audit
$1.2M Annual labor cost savings

Controls Implemented

  • RBAC with analyst/supervisor/auditor roles
  • All agent actions logged to immutable audit store
  • Human approval required for escalation decisions
  • Daily evaluation runs against golden test cases
  • Model risk documentation per SR 11-7

Insurance Claims Processing

P&C Insurer Automates 40% of Claims Intake

Document extraction and triage automation with human-in-the-loop for complex claims

The Challenge

A mid-sized property and casualty insurer was struggling with claims intake efficiency. Adjusters spent significant time on initial document review and data entry before they could begin actual claim assessment.

The company processed 15,000+ claims monthly across auto, property, and commercial lines. Each claim arrived with varying document types and formats, making traditional OCR solutions insufficient.

The Solution

We built an agentic claims intake system that processes incoming documents, extracts relevant information, validates against policy data, and routes claims to appropriate adjusters with preliminary assessments.

  • Multi-format document processing (PDF, images, emails)
  • Policy lookup and coverage verification
  • Automated complexity scoring for routing
  • Structured data extraction to claims system
  • Adjuster review queue with agent recommendations

Results

40% Claims processed automatically
72% Reduction in intake time
94% Data extraction accuracy
2.1 days Faster first contact (avg)

Controls Implemented

  • Confidence thresholds for automatic vs. manual review
  • PII handling with field-level encryption
  • Audit logs for regulatory compliance
  • Weekly accuracy evaluation against adjuster decisions
  • Escalation paths for ambiguous documents

SaaS / IT Services IT Helpdesk

Enterprise SaaS Vendor Deflects 35% of Tier-1 Tickets

Knowledge-powered agent for internal IT support with escalation intelligence

The Challenge

A B2B SaaS company with 2,500 employees was overwhelmed with internal IT support requests. Their helpdesk team of 8 was spending most of their time on repetitive Tier-1 issues like password resets, software access requests, and common troubleshooting.

Previous chatbot implementations had failed because they couldn't handle the variety of requests or take actual actions. Employees had learned to bypass the bot and go straight to human support.

The Solution

We deployed an agentic IT support assistant that can understand requests, retrieve relevant documentation, and take approved actions like password resets and access provisioning—with appropriate guardrails.

  • RAG over IT knowledge base with freshness tracking
  • Integration with Okta for identity operations
  • ServiceNow ticket creation and updates
  • Slack-native interface for employee access
  • Smart escalation with context handoff to humans

Results

35% Ticket deflection rate
4.2/5 Employee satisfaction score
< 2 min Avg resolution time (deflected)
2 FTEs Capacity freed for Tier-2/3

Controls Implemented

  • Action allowlist (only approved operations)
  • Manager approval for access grants above threshold
  • Rate limiting per user and operation type
  • Full conversation logging for security review
  • Weekly false-positive/negative analysis

B2B Services Email Triage

Logistics Provider Automates 60% of Customer Email Routing

Intelligent email classification and response drafting for operations team

The Challenge

A regional logistics company received 500+ customer emails daily across shipment status, rate quotes, claims, and operational issues. A team of 6 customer service reps manually triaged and responded to each email, often taking hours to route complex requests.

Response time was suffering, with average first response taking 4+ hours. The operations team needed to prioritize urgent shipment issues but had no systematic way to identify them in the inbox flood.

The Solution

We built an email triage agent that classifies incoming emails, extracts key information, drafts responses for common queries, and routes complex issues to the right team member with context.

  • Multi-label classification (type, urgency, account tier)
  • Entity extraction (shipment IDs, dates, locations)
  • TMS lookup for real-time shipment status
  • Response drafting with human approval
  • Priority queue for time-sensitive issues

Results

60% Emails auto-classified and routed
78% Draft acceptance rate
< 1 hr Avg first response time (from 4+ hrs)
23% Increase in customer NPS

Controls Implemented

  • Human approval required before sending any response
  • Escalation rules for high-value accounts
  • Classification confidence thresholds
  • Weekly accuracy review with CS team
  • Feedback loop for continuous improvement
