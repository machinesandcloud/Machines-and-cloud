Our Mission

Close the gap between AI promise and production reality

Enterprise AI spending is accelerating, but most agentic projects stall between "impressive demo" and "production deployment." The gap isn't technology—it's engineering discipline.

We exist to close that gap. We bring the integration craftsmanship, governance controls, and operational rigor that turns AI experiments into production systems.

Our clients don't need another AI vendor promising transformation. They need engineers who can ship systems that work, with controls that satisfy security teams, and metrics that justify continued investment.

What We Believe

  • Agents are software systems, not magic
  • Production-ready means governance-ready
  • One workflow, one outcome beats platform promises
  • Integration is the work, not an afterthought
  • Speed-to-value beats scope maximization
  • Measurable KPIs beat impressive demos
What We Do

Agentic workflow automation, done right

We design, build, and operate AI agents that automate knowledge work—with the governance controls that enterprise buyers require.

Discovery & Planning

Map workflows, identify automation candidates, define KPIs, and assess data readiness. No building until success metrics are signed.

Agent Engineering

Build agentic workflows with real integrations, approval gates, and evaluation harnesses. Production architecture from day one.

Security & Governance

Implement OWASP LLM risk mitigations, NIST AI RMF alignment, audit trails, and approval workflows. Built-in, not bolted-on.

Production Launch

Deploy to production with monitoring, runbooks, and incident playbooks. SLOs defined, rollback plan ready, team trained.

Ongoing Operations

Monitor, optimize, and expand. Drift detection, cost management, evaluation regression testing, and incremental workflow rollouts.

Continuous Improvement

Monthly KPI reporting, quarterly roadmap planning, and systematic expansion to additional workflows. Not one-time delivery.

Why Us

What makes us different

Engineering, Not Experimentation

We treat agentic automation as a software engineering discipline, not an AI experiment. That means version control, testing, deployment pipelines, monitoring, and incident response—the same rigor you expect from any production system.

Governance-First

Security and compliance aren't afterthoughts. Every engagement includes OWASP LLM risk mitigation, audit trails, approval workflows, and evaluation harnesses. We satisfy security teams, not just business sponsors.

Vendor-Portable

We don't lock you into proprietary platforms. Our systems work across model providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, AWS, Google) and integrate with your existing stack. When models improve or prices change, you can adapt.

Outcome-Driven Packages

Every engagement has defined triggers, deliverables, and acceptance criteria. Fixed-fee packages where possible, milestone-based pricing for launches. You pay for outcomes, not hours.
