FAQ

Common questions

How long does a typical engagement take?

An Opportunity Sprint takes 7-10 days. Proof-of-Value builds take 4-6 weeks. Production launches typically run 8-12 weeks depending on integration complexity.

What industries do you work with?

We focus on financial services, insurance, SaaS/IT, retail, manufacturing, and B2B services—verticals with high-value knowledge work and clear metrics.

How do you handle security and compliance?

Every engagement includes OWASP LLM Top 10 risk mitigation, audit trails, and governance controls. We align with NIST AI RMF and can support SOC 2, HIPAA, and other frameworks.

What's your pricing model?

Sprint and PoV builds are fixed-fee. Production launches are milestone-based. Retainers are monthly with defined scope. Model/API costs are passed through transparently.

Do you require long-term contracts?

No. Each package has defined deliverables and acceptance criteria. You can start with a Sprint and decide whether to continue based on results.

Can you work with our existing AI investments?

Yes. We're vendor-portable and work with OpenAI, Anthropic, AWS Bedrock, Azure OpenAI, and Google. We integrate with platforms like Copilot Studio and ServiceNow.
