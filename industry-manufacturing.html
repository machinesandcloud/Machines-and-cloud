The Challenge

Downtime costs compound by the minute

When equipment fails, every minute of delay in diagnosis and parts procurement multiplies production losses.

Work Order Backlog

Planners spend hours routing work orders to the right crews. Priority conflicts and resource constraints delay critical repairs.

Parts Lookup Friction

Technicians search through multiple systems to find parts. Legacy SKUs, obsolete catalogs, and manual cross-references waste time.

Tribal Knowledge Loss

Experienced technicians retiring take decades of equipment knowledge with them. New hires take years to reach full productivity.

Documentation Burden

QA requirements demand detailed documentation. Technicians spend more time on paperwork than repairs.
