Privacy policy

This page explains what information Machines & Cloud collects through this website, how we use it, and how to contact us about privacy questions.

Information we collect

  • Contact details you submit through forms, scheduling links, or direct email
  • Business information you share when discussing workflows, requirements, or project scope
  • Basic usage and analytics information collected through site measurement tools, if enabled

How we use information

  • Respond to inquiries and schedule discovery or follow-up conversations
  • Evaluate potential project fit, workflow scope, and service requirements
  • Improve site content, performance, and business communications

How we share information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers involved in site hosting, scheduling, analytics, communications, or project delivery when that sharing is necessary to operate the business.

Retention and security

We retain business inquiry and communication records for as long as needed to manage relationships, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and maintain business records. We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards appropriate to the nature of the information involved.

Your choices

You can contact us to request updates or deletion of information you previously shared, subject to legal and contractual obligations. You can also choose not to submit optional information through the website.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact support@machinesandcloud.com or write to Machines & Cloud, 5718 Westheimer Rd Suite 1000, Houston, TX 77057.
