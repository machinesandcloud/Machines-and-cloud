The Challenge

Communication overload kills service quality

B2B service teams drown in emails, tickets, and document requests. Response delays erode client relationships and create churn risk.

Inbox Overload

500+ emails daily across shared inboxes. Critical client requests buried under routine inquiries. Nothing is auto-triaged.

Response Delays

Average first response takes 4+ hours. Clients expect faster. Every hour of delay increases churn risk.

CRM Data Decay

Client interactions scattered across email, calls, and meetings. CRM never gets updated. Account context is incomplete.

Document Chaos

Contracts, invoices, and reports arrive via email. Manual filing is inconsistent. Finding the right document takes forever.
