Buyer Personas

The stakeholder coalition

A production agentic system requires a coalition, not a single buyer. We know how to navigate the economic buyer, technical buyer, and risk gatekeepers.

Economic Buyer

COO / VP Operations / Head of Customer Support

Owns KPI + budget. Cares about: "Cycle time down, cost-to-serve down, SLA adherence up—tracked weekly."

Technical Buyer

CIO / CTO / VP Engineering

Owns architecture decisions. Cares about: "Works with your stack; secure tool permissions; no new fragile platform."

Risk Gatekeepers

CISO / Legal / Compliance

Can veto. Cares about: "Threat model + OWASP LLM risk mitigations + audit trails; aligns to NIST AI RMF."

Day-to-Day Champion

Process Owner / Ops Manager

Drives adoption. Cares about: "Exception handling stays human; approvals at risk points; clear handoff rules."
