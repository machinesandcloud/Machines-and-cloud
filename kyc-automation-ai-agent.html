KYC automation with an AI agent

A KYC automation agent should gather evidence, assemble case summaries, surface policy conflicts, and route approvals without hiding the review trail or overstepping regulatory controls.

See Financial ServicesSee Governance Controls

Good fit

  • High case volume
  • Multi-system research steps
  • Known review criteria
  • Strict audit expectations

Agent responsibilities

  • Collect approved internal and vendor data
  • Generate a cited case summary
  • Flag missing or conflicting evidence
  • Route exceptions to review

Controls

  • No autonomous escalation without approval
  • Full evidence and action logs
  • Role-based data access
  • Golden-set testing for edge cases
